    ABOUT ME

    Hi! My name is Inna. For me, graphic design is a chance to share my perspective on the world with other people. Through commercial logos, brands and web sites, I try to take you into my personal imagination. I am influenced both by traditional arts such as Russian animation and modern design trends. I've graduated from Bezalel Academy of Arts in Visual Communication and currently live in Tel Aviv. Enjoy my portfolio.
